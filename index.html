Why?

The space debris problem

Why a blockchain?

Space Economy is fragile

Billions of dollars of space assets contribute trillions of dollars to the economy. A strategic collision event can bring down the whole space infrastructure, making it unusable for future generations.

Whom to trust?

Space assets tracking is fragmented over various players, making it difficult in a conflict to ensure safe use of space. Actionable positional information about space assets and debris can be spoofed or completely obscured.

The global space-faring community needs to address the debris issue, before the orbital environment becomes unusable.
— Stephane Germain, CEO, GHGSat
Protecting the space environment is of utmost importance to humanity's short-term and long-term success.
— Will Marshall, CEO and Co-Founder, Planet

VISION

Decentralized Space Safety

In peace and in conflict, a sustainable space ecosystem requires trustless mechanisms that integrate data from various sources to generate accurate and verifiable conjunction analysis.

Space is for everyone. Tracking space based assets through independent sources and trustless aggregation ensures that no single player can trigger the Kessler syndrome.

We integrate various sources of information, generate consensus over a blockchain and produce tracking and conjunction information that can be publicly accessed and verified in a trustless manner.
